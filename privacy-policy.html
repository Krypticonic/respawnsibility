Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 23, 2025

ReSPAWNsibility ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Zombie Unknown mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is essential for the core functionality of our game. The data we collect is limited to:

  • Game Progress Data: This includes your high scores, achievements, unlocked items, and other gameplay statistics. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Leaderboard Data: If you choose to submit your score to the global leaderboards, we will collect your chosen player name and your score. This is the only information that is transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the Service, primarily the global leaderboard feature.
  • To allow you to track your progress and achievements within the game.

Data Storage and Security

Your core game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it. Leaderboard data (player name and score) is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable measures to protect this information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email: respawnsibilitygames@gmail.com.
