Game Features

Intense Arcade Survival

Dive into pure, adrenaline-fueled action. With no story to slow you down, it's just you against an ever-growing horde. Every second counts as you push for a new high score.

Evolving Arsenal & Abilities

Adapt or die. Collect powerful buffs like Overcharge and Damage Boost. Unlock new weapons and master game-changing abilities like the Phase Dash to turn the tide of battle.

Compete on Global Leaderboards

You may be fighting alone, but you're competing with the world. Submit your score and see how you stack up against other survivors on the global leaderboards. Claim your spot at the top.

About ReSPAWNsibility

We are ReSPAWNsibility, a husband-and-wife indie team on a mission to create games we love to play. Our name is our philosophy: taking responsibility for crafting fun, polished experiences and breathing new life into our creative ideas.

Our portfolio is diverse, ranging from intense arcade shooters like Zombie Unknown to charming educational games for the whole family. This allows us to explore different genres and connect with a wide audience of players. Zombie Unknown is our adrenaline-fueled love letter to the horde survival genre, and we can't wait for you to jump in and chase that next high score.
